# Fate Primer — Authoring Notes

## Before You Start

- Read the game primers skill\arcs-rules\primer-base-game.md and skill\arcs-rules\primer-expansion.md in full before writing
- Read the relevant Void Chroniclers fate guide in full before writing

## Format

**Opening:** A few sentences on the fate's identity, general approach, and core mechanics. Not a summary of the guide — a characterisation that orients the reader.

**Per act:** Cover each act with:
- Which part of the objective to focus on, and how to achieve it
- The most important kit items (with a brief note on why each matters)
- Notable matchups with other fates

Include the **difficulty and complexity ratings** (e.g. Complexity: 2/5, Difficulty: 3/5) for each act, taken from the guide.

## Style

- Bullets over prose unless there are nuances that need explaining.
- No more than a page per act.
- Don't repeat points across acts.
- Be succinct — assume the reader has played the game.
